Title: InternChain: A Blockchain-Based Internship Monitoring and Performance Verification System
Description
InternChain is a system that records internship activities, attendance, and supervisor evaluations on a blockchain. It ensures that internship records cannot be altered and provides a trusted verification system for schools and companies.
